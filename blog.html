Chapter Wars

National athletics event!

Nine will enter -- only one will leave victorious. Defend your chapter's honor through some friendly competition with the brothers of our fraternity.

Every year, Nu Alpha Phi organizes a sports olympic, brother against brother in sports like softball, basketball, and volleyball between all nine chapters. Naphis from every generation, young and old, practice to prepare themselves for this annual event.

When chapter pride is on the line, it is the duty of every brother to demonstrate the strength of his own pack. Only one will earn the crown.

TASA Nightmarket

Experience Taiwanese Culture!

Is Pusheen your spirit animal? Does Totoro's fuzzy belly make your heart melt? Do you own an excessive number of Tsum Tsums? Inspired by the animated characters that helped make Kawaii (or "cuteness") a worldwide trend, TASA and Rutgers Nu Alpha Phi proudly present our Nightmarket: Kawaii Avenue!! That's right... we're back at it again with our 14th annual Nightmarket, the biggest TASA event of the spring semester!!

NAPhinest

Compete to see who's the Phinest

Help us make you a star or watch in awe while Naphi Brothers provide awareness and raise money for wounded troops and veterans. PROCEEDS will be donated to the Wounded Warrior Project, a charity service organization empowering injured veterans and their families. Their mission is to honor and empower Wounded Warriors.
